Walk-A-Hunt: Walk for Depression Awareness

DON'T WALK ALONE, WALK WITH US!

19th July 2019, 8:00am to 11:00am

Taipan, USJ 10

Come and join us at "Walk-A-Hunt: Walk For Depression Awareness" - a treasure hunt event that aims to raise awareness and de-stigmatise depression.

Each team will have to WALK around Taipan, USJ10, where they must compete to solve puzzles based on street signages and to answer questions relating to mental health and depression.

An exhibition by professionals, support groups and non-government organisations (NGOs) related to mental health will be presented, to spread awareness of depression and educate the community on where to seek help.
