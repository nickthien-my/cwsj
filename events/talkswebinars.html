Finding Order in The Midst of Chaos

7th August 2021, 9pm to 10pm

Over the past year, the Covid-19 pandemic has transformed nearly every aspect of our world. Social isolation, working from home, loss of jobs, and having to juggle between multiple roles at home can be challenging.Furthermore, working from home can lead to a distorted sense of time. All these can lead to anxiety and stress.

This week, we have a special guest -- Dr. Jasimah Jamir (Psychiatric Senior Registrar, Dublin, Ireland) who has vast clinical experience in psychiatry and is trained for various psychotherapies, to share with us essential techniques to put our lives back in order.
